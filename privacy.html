Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we collect and handle your data.

At AllFonts, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may share while visiting our site. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect basic usage data such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring site. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to browse our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for improving the user experience, analyzing traffic, and securing the platform. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to analyze site traffic and enhance performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer not to be tracked.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to external sites (e.g., font sources or social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and recommend reading their policies separately.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of any personal data we may have (if applicable). To make a request, please contact us through our Contact page.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We recommend reviewing it regularly.
