Affiliate Disclosure

How we support our site through affiliate links — transparently and honestly.

Some of the links on AllFonts may be affiliate links. This means that if you click on one of those links and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Running and maintaining a free resource like AllFonts takes time, effort, and hosting costs. By partnering with trusted affiliate programs, we can keep the site free for users while still earning a small income to support ongoing improvements.

What It Means for You

Clicking on an affiliate link does not affect your purchase price in any way. In fact, we only recommend tools, products, and font sources that we truly believe add value to your creative process.

Our Promise

Transparency matters to us. We will always be clear about affiliate links, and you can trust that any recommendations made on AllFonts are based on real usability and quality — not commissions.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate links or how we earn through them, feel free to contact us. We're happy to explain.
